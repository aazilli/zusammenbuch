About

Welcome to Das Zusammenbuch, a dashboard created for the German-English translator so that they might be spared some fraction of the agony involved in opening endless browser tabs for each of their many dictionary needs. View the results of multiple dictionaries with a single search! Enlist or cull dictionaries on a whim via the settings page! Display results in your preferred order!

As of now there are no immediate plans to expand this tool to other languages, but there will be additional English and German-language dictionaries coming soon.
